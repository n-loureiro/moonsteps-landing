About

Moonsteps was founded by Nuno Loureiro, a physicist and neuroscientist working at the intersection of AI, data engineering, and investigative analysis.

Projects combine research-grade machine learning with production-ready data systems — built for organizations that require solutions designed for their specific domain complexity.

Moonsteps works best where data is messy, fragmented, or unstructured — where existing tools fall short and custom engineering is the only path forward.
